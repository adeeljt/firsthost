Dr. Adeel Arif

Currently I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Science at University of Central Punjab and Founder & Head of Research Group LahoriBot.

I worked as an Assistant Professor at Faculty of Information Technology at University of Central Punjab Lahore and Founder & Director of Center of Robotics and Security from 2016-2017.

I worked as an Assistant Professor at Computer Science department at FAST National University Lahore and Founding Director of Liberty Lab for Robotics and Embedded Systems from 2013-2015.

Before this, I was living in the city of Shanghai doing a Doctrate at the Machine Learning and Robotics (MLR) group at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China.

I completed my PhD from University of Tuebingen, Germany, in the Robotics Research Group. My work was mostly on Visual Terrain Classification onboard wheeled and flying robots.

Previously, I was at Kaiserslautern University of Technology, Germany for a few years involved in teaching and research at Robotics and AI Group and Image Understanding and Pattern Recognition (IUPR) Group at DFKI.

I did BS computer science from FAST - National University of Computer & Emerging Sciences.
