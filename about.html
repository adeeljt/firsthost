About

Dr. Adeel Arif is a recognized leader in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Computer Science education. He is the Founder & Head of Research Group LahoriBot, and an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Central Punjab. Dr. Arif has authored numerous research papers in machine learning, robotics, and related fields. He has held key academic and research positions at leading universities and has contributed significantly to the advancement of AI and robotics in Pakistan and internationally.

As a pioneer in robotics and online education, Dr. Arif has changed countless lives through his work in AI, and was named to the list of most influential AI persons in the region. He holds degrees from top institutions and is passionate about empowering the next generation of AI researchers and engineers.
